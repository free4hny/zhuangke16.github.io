Project Introduction

This is my year 2 short semester design project. We were asked to collaborate with a very diverse team - a combination of Telecommunication Engineering students(my sub-group, responsible for hardware), E-commerce Engineering students(responsible for shopping website), and Internet of Things Engineering students(responsible for database).

The basic requirements of the hardware is to own two kinds of working mode, "lamp" and "night light".
The "lamp" mode: ① manually switch; ② manually adjust brightness; ③ users with less than 20 cm distance from the lamp will send out sound and light alarm; ④ can turn off the light automatically preset time, at the same time automatically into "night light" mode.
The "night light" mode: ① switch off in the bright environment; ② in the dark environment, when the distance between user and the lamp is less than 30 cm, the brightness of the lamp automatically to weak light; ③ the light lasts 1 min and turn off after if the distance between user and the lamp more than 30 cm; ④ can be set to automatically turn on the light in advance(preset) time, at the same time automatically entered into "lamp" mode.
